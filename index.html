About Us

John Craig

- Head Trainer

John Craig is a lifelong Muay Thai practioner that started from a very young age. When he was a teenager he moved to Thailand to get proper tradional training in the ways of Muay Thai, After many years of training and fighting in Thailand he decided to bring his newfound knowledge back to Scotland.

Alison McDonald

- Senior Trainer

Alison has trained for many years under the watchful eye of John and is now in her own right an expert of Muay Thai. After a long career and many fights, she now takes the women's classes at EK Fight Gym.
